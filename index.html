The Founding Moment

There Are People Who
Study Business. There Are
People Who Teach It.
CA Manan Vasa Is Neither.

Mumbai. A machine parts manufacturing unit. An exhausted founder — mid-fifties, reading glasses pushed up on his forehead, phone in one hand, purchase order in the other — simultaneously approving a vendor payment, answering a sales call and explaining to a junior why a delivery had been delayed.

Twelve people working for him. He was doing the work of all twelve. This man had attended programs. He had read the books. He knew exactly what needed to change. And yet — at 6 PM on a Wednesday — he was still the first person in and the last person out.

"It was not a knowledge problem. It was not a motivation problem. It was the absence of one thing — someone willing to stay until it was fixed. What if instead of telling him what to do — I stayed until it was done?"

That question became the foundation of Altus Corp — and the rule that has held for 21 years:

Frequently Asked

Every founder asks these before they apply. Read them carefully — not because they answer every question, but because they ask the right ones.

How do I categorise what CA Manan Vasa does?
+
You cannot — because the category does not exist yet. He is not a business coach, consultant, advisor, trainer, motivational speaker, or Virtual CFO. He is a Business Transformation Architect — someone who enters your business, diagnoses it completely and honestly, designs a transformation across all six operational domains (foundation, strategy, structure, processes, people, performance) and implements it alongside you. He does not leave until it is done.
Why does it matter that he is a Chartered Accountant?
+
Because a CA reads numbers the way a surgeon reads a scan. Most business transformation work is done by people who cannot read a P&L with clinical precision. Manan can see what is broken in a business from its financials alone — before he even enters it. His CA foundation means the transformation is grounded in financial reality, not motivation or theory. Every recommendation is anchored in what the numbers say and what the numbers need to say.
How is this different from everything I have tried before?
+
Everything you have tried before ended when the event or engagement ended. You were handed a plan, a framework, a report — and then left alone to implement it. That is why you are still in the same place. The difference here is simple: we do not leave. We build the systems alongside you. We hold accountability every single week. We have one dedicated Implementer for you alone. We are not complete until results are visible in your business. There is no "on your own" in this engagement.
Why are you limited to only 20 founders?
+
Because depth is not possible at scale. And depth is the only thing that actually changes a business. With 20 founders, each one gets a dedicated Implementer who knows their business intimately. Each one gets direct access to CA Manan Vasa. Each one gets 100% customised work — not a generic framework adapted for their notes. The moment the number exceeds 20, depth becomes impossible. That rule has held for 21 years. It will not change.
What happens in the first conversation?
+
CA Manan Vasa will not try to sell you. He will ask about your business — its structure, its numbers, its people, its challenges. He will tell you honestly whether this engagement is right for you. He will also tell you if it is not. He does not take engagements he cannot win. The first conversation is a genuine assessment — not a pitch.
Can I speak to someone who has worked with him?
+
Yes — and we encourage it. Over 1,500 businesses have worked with CA Manan Vasa. You can read documented case studies, watch video testimonials, and read LinkedIn recommendations. If you want a direct conversation with a past participant, request it in your application and we will connect you.
How involved is CA Manan Vasa personally versus his team?

Manan leads every Monday group session personally. He is available on WhatsApp (+91 80970 10410) for direct access. Your dedicated Implementer — trained by Manan on 4,200+ real business projects — handles the weekly one-to-one sessions. Is this right for a family business?

Yes — family businesses are some of the highest-impact engagements.
