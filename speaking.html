5 Keynote Topics

Five Talks. Zero Theory.
Everything From the Ground.

01
Signature Keynote
#NoGyaanOnlyGain: Why Knowledge Never Changes a Business — And What Does
The most confronting talk CA Manan Vasa gives. Why you already know everything you need to know — and it has changed nothing. The structural difference between knowledge and implementation. What actually moves numbers in a business. Why the next book, seminar or consultant will produce the same result as the last one.
45 – 90 Minutes
Founders, Business Owners, Senior Professionals, CA Firms
02
Keynote + Workshop
The Founder Trap: How to Build a Business That Runs Without You
The most requested talk for MSME and SME founders. What founder dependency actually is — and why it is structural, not personal. The six domains where it lives. The exact sequence to eliminate it. Why most solutions only fix one domain and leave the other five intact. What a business that runs without the founder actually looks like — and what it takes to build one.
60 – 90 Minutes
MSME and SME Founders, Family Business Events
03
Keynote
The IKIGAI of Business Transformation: Why One Expert Is Never Enough
Why your business simultaneously experiences six domains — foundation, strategy, structure, processes, people, and performance — and why fixing only one always fails. The IKIGAI framework visualised for business. Why specialists are incomplete solutions. What the intersection looks like — and what it produces.
45 – 60 Minutes
Founders, Investors, Strategy Events

Keynote
Family Business Succession: The Mistakes Most Founders Make
The three mistakes that destroy family business succession — and the one structural approach that works. Drawn from direct experience across multiple family business transitions. The governance gap. The communication gap. The authority gap. How to close all three without destroying the relationship in the process.

Family Business Founders, Next-Generation Leaders
