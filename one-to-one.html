What This Is

Private. Bespoke.
Intensive. No Shared Attention.

The One-to-One Business Transformation is not the BSS™ cohort. It is a completely different engagement — more intensive, more private, more customised. CA Manan Vasa works directly and exclusively alongside one founder. Your business. Your complexity. His complete focus.

He does not divide his attention. He does not apply a framework built for twenty. He enters your specific business, diagnoses it completely and honestly, designs a transformation architecture unique to your situation, and implements it alongside you until it is done.

"Not consulting. Not advisory. Not coaching. A private, one-to-one Business Implementation Intervention — where the only outcome that matters is the one visible in your business."

This engagement is for situations where the complexity, the stakes, or the sensitivity of the transformation demands complete privacy and undivided attention. Family business succession. Second-generation transitions. Turnarounds. Restructuring. High-stakes strategic pivots.
