Case Study 04 · Logistics
SM Express Logistics Pvt Ltd
₹9.6 Cr Overdue → ₹3 Cr · 60 Days · ₹2 Cr Bad Debt Fully Recovered
Timeline: 60 Days
60 Days
To Recover ₹9.6 Cr Overdue
The Problem

₹9.6 Cr in overdue outstanding. 86% aged over 120 days. ₹2 Cr declared bad debt — written off, assumed gone. No standardised collections process. The founder personally chasing payments — an exhausting and undignified position.

The Intervention

Standard collections process designed from scratch. Aging tracking system installed. Internal accountability framework built. Bad debt recovery process created. Founder deliberately removed from routine collections — dignity and bandwidth restored.

The Results
  • Overdue: ₹9.6 Cr → ₹3 Cr in 60 days
  • Aged 120+ days: 86% → 34%
  • ₹2 Cr "bad debt" — fully recovered
  • Founder removed from routine collections
  • Collections now system-driven, not personality-driven

"Brought down outstanding of over 120 days from 9.6 Cr to 3 Cr in 60 days. From 86% outstanding above 120 days we brought it down to 34%. Recovered 2 Cr that was declared bad debts in our books for over 18 months."

Case Study 05 · Industrial Manufacturing
Sunniva Encom LLP
471% Growth · From Importer to Cost-Leader Manufacturer · Now Exporting
Timeline: FY 2021–22
471%
Growth in FY 2021-22
The Problem

Importer in a market where Chinese competition was eroding margins with every passing year. A structurally vulnerable business model — entirely dependent on import arbitrage that was disappearing. No clear path to survival, let alone growth.

The Intervention

Full strategic transformation — manufacturing transition designed as a structured project. Financial model built. Process architecture designed. Team structure created. Cost model engineered to produce below Chinese import prices. The entire business model redesigned from the ground up.

The Results
  • 471% growth in FY 2021–22

  • From importer to cost-leader manufacturer
  • Now producing below Chinese import cost
  • Now exporting
